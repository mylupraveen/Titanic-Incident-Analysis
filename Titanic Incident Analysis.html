ANALYSIS OF TITANIC INCIDENT¶

INTRODUCTORY:¶

TITANIC was an British passenger liner which was collided with an iceberg during her maiden voyage from Southampton to Newyork city resulting in sink to the bottom of North Atlantic Ocean in the year 1912. Approximately 2224 passengers were aboard the ship. These ship includes embarkation point in Cherbourg and Queenstown. We are going to anlayze these incident with a dataset of 891 passengers.

DATA DICTIONARY:

survival - Survival
pclass - Ticket class

KEY:

survival:
0 = NO
1 = YES

Pclass: 1 = 1st, 2 = 2nd, 3 = 3rd

port: C = Cherbourg, Q = Queenstown, S = Southampton
